MY BIO

Richard Addo-Owusu is a Pharmacy Student and Aspiring Data Scientist with a keen interest in leveraging data-driven solutions to improve healthcare outcomes. With a strong foundation in both pharmacy and data analytics, Richard is committed to bridging the gap between technology and healthcare to drive meaningful impact in the industry.
